Open World Exploration

Journey across vast landscapes, each full of secrets, dangers, and opportunities. Discover hidden locations, face off against mythical creatures, and uncover long-forgotten histories.

Crafting and Building

Gather resources from the environment to craft weapons, armor, and tools that will help you survive and thrive. Build your own base to protect yourself from hostile forces or simply to call it home.

Multiplayer Co-op

Team up with friends or brave adventurers from around the globe. Work together to build, explore, and survive—or choose to go rogue and create your own path.

Survival and Combat

Every decision matters. Hunt for food, gather resources, and craft powerful weapons to defend yourself from fierce monsters and other players. Fight in both large-scale battles and intimate encounters in real-time combat.

Dark Fantasy Setting

Immerse yourself in a world inspired by Dungeons & Dragons, with creatures, magic, and lore that will challenge your wits and willpower. Encounter dark forces that will test your courage at every turn.

About the Developer

Hello, my name is Christoph Fuchs! I'm a solo indie developer with a deep passion for fantasy worlds and all their fascinating aspects. As a self-taught individual, I've acquired the skills to bring projects like Wyversium to life.

It fills me with joy to create immersive experiences that transport players to fantastical realms. Wyversium is a project close to my heart, combining my love for Dungeons & Dragons, survival games, and of course, dark fantasy.
