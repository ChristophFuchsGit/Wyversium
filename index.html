Core pillars

What is Wyversium?

A dark, wyvern-filled fantasy world. Survive, build, and carve out your story in a hostile land where every night can be your last.

Exploration

Open world discovery

Travel through towering cliffs, mist-soaked forests and forgotten temples. Hunt for resources and uncover hidden stories scattered across Wyversium.

Survival

Craft, build, endure

Gather, craft and build your stronghold. Fortify your base against roaming creatures and unpredictable threats stalking the night.

Wyverns

Ride the skies

Seek out wyvern nests, tame your own beast, and command the skies. Your wyvern can be your greatest ally – or your biggest risk.

Co-op

Survive together

Team up with friends to build shared bases, raid dangerous areas, and take on challenges that no lone survivor could handle.

The world

A D&D-styled dark fantasy realm

Wyversium blends tabletop-inspired fantasy with survival gameplay – brutal, atmospheric and driven by your choices.

From shattered ruins to wyvern-infested canyons, every region of Wyversium hides both danger and opportunity. Food, shelter and allies are scarce – and the deeper you venture, the more the world pushes back.

Ancient magic lingers in relics, altars and forgotten paths. Some may grant power. Others may wake something better left undisturbed.

What to expect
  • Ark-like survival with wyvern-centered gameplay.
  • D&D-inspired dark fantasy atmosphere and lore.
  • Exploration-driven progression with secrets and hidden paths.
  • Base-building, crafting and gear progression.
  • Solo or co-op – carve your own story.

Developer

Who is behind Wyversium?

A solo indie developer building a world where wyverns, survival and dark fantasy collide.

Hi, I’m Christoph Fuchs – a solo indie dev with a long-running obsession for fantasy worlds, wyverns and survival games. Wyversium is my attempt to bring that passion to life.

The game blends my love for Dungeons & Dragons, Ark-style survival and dark fantasy storytelling. Every system is built with that mix in mind: harsh, atmospheric and full of choices that matter.

If you want to follow development more closely, join the Discord – that’s where I share updates, ask for feedback and sometimes break things live.
